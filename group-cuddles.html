Facilitated Group Cuddles

Guided group experiences that foster connection, communication, and community.

Learn about boundaries, consent, and the power of platonic touch in a supportive group setting. These facilitated sessions bring together a small group of people in a safe, structured environment where you can practice communication, explore different forms of touch, and experience the warmth of community connection. Each session includes guidance on consent practices, boundary-setting, and creating a respectful space for everyone.

Details

Group Size

Typically 6-12 participants per session

Pricing

  • $40-$60 per person per session
  • Sliding scale available

Location

Held in a comfortable, spacious venue. Location details provided upon registration.

Duration

Sessions typically last 2-3 hours, including orientation and closing circle

Scheduling

Regular monthly sessions. Check the calendar for upcoming dates or contact to schedule a private group.

Guidelines

  • All participants must agree to group guidelines and code of conduct
  • Consent is required for all touch interactions
  • You can participate at whatever level feels comfortable
